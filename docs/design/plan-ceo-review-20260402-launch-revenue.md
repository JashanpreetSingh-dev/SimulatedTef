## Vision

### 10x Check
TEF Canada becomes the default recommendation in every language school's TEF prep course — "use this app alongside class." 200+ paying subscribers at $25-45/mo, partner codes distributed by 20+ schools, Exam Readiness Score that replaces tutor progress checks. MRR: $4,000-8,000/mo from individual subs. B2B school licenses are a future horizon, not this sprint.

### Why this is achievable
The market is fragmented, the AI-native wedge is real (Gemini Live oral examiner simulation has no direct competitor), and the B2B infrastructure is already built. The bottleneck right now is distribution, not product.

## Accepted Scope (added to this plan)

- **Language school outreach:** Identify 5-10 TEF prep language schools (Alliance Française branches, private schools). Email them a partner discount code (30% off, generated via Stripe dashboard). Track redemptions per code. See email template below.
- **Day 1 onboarding fix:** First-time users see a "Start your first oral practice" banner on Dashboard.tsx. Trigger: `hasCompletedFirstPractice === false` on first login. CTA links directly to Section A oral practice. Dismisses permanently after user completes their first session (not on close).
- **Activation metric:** Log `first_practice_completed` event in LogRocket when a user finishes their first oral Section A or B session. Anchor on oral (not writing/reading) — oral is the #1 reason users subscribe. Track activation rate (signups → first oral practice) as leading conversion indicator.
- **Landing page hero rewrite:** Pricing context: Basic $25/mo (10 oral attempts), Premium $45/mo (30 oral attempts). New framing: "One month of premium ($45) = less than one hour of TEF tutoring. Practice 30 oral sessions with an AI examiner — on your schedule." Target: skilled immigrant, 2-3 months from TEF exam, has basic French.

## 60-Day Sequenced Sprint Plan

Sequenced for a solo founder. One thing at a time.

### Days 1-5: Fix before distributing (build)
1. **Verify Stripe promo codes** — test a 30%-off coupon end-to-end on a test account before generating real codes (10min)
2. **Generate discount codes** — create 10 unique 30%-off promo codes in Stripe dashboard (30min)
3. **Onboarding fix** — add first-time user banner to Dashboard.tsx (2-3hr)
4. **Activation metric** — add `first_practice_completed` LogRocket event (1hr)
5. **Landing page hero** — rewrite to tutor replacement framing with pricing (1hr)

### Week 1: Reddit first (fastest signal)
- Post one genuine answer in r/ImmigrationCanada or r/CanadaVisa about TEF oral prep
- Include UTM link: `?utm_source=reddit&utm_medium=community`
- Watch LogRocket for signups + activation rate
- Do NOT launch Facebook or schools yet — get one data point first

### Week 2: Language school outreach
- Email 5-10 TEF prep schools using the template above
- Track which codes get redeemed
- Join 2-3 Facebook Express Entry groups (lurk and build history this week — don't post yet)

### Week 3-4: Content + measure
- Write Reddit founder story: "I couldn't find good TEF Canada oral practice tools so I built one — here's what I learned about Section B." Include: what the exam format actually is, how AI practice works, honest limitations, soft mention of the app. Post in r/ImmigrationCanada.
- Review LogRocket: activation rate, UTM attribution, drop-off
- Follow up with schools that opened the email
- Post first piece of value content in Facebook groups (TEF prep tip, not a pitch)

### Week 5-8: Double down on best channel
- Identify best-converting channel from data (UTM attribution + LogRocket)
- Reddit: post 2x/week in relevant threads if converting. If not, stop.
- Schools: follow up with all non-responders. Offer 1 free demo call.
- Facebook: post once/week value content if groups are showing clicks
- Begin immigration consultant outreach if school channel shows 3+ redemptions

## Success Criteria
- 10 new paying users within 60 days
- Activation rate (signup → first oral practice) > 40%
- At least 1 school discount code redeemed (not "partnership" — just first redemption)
- Best-converting channel identified by week 5
